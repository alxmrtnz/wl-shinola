The Runwell

$550

Built to last, and named accordingly, a timepiece built for life from the finest components available in the world. Features of The Runwell include a sapphire crystal, Super-LumiNova printed dial details, and a solid stainless steel case with screw down crown.

Two hands and remote second hand sweep are driven by Shinola's Argonite 1069 high-accuracy quartz movement. From the laser-etched case back to the etched lightning bolt on the buckle, every detail of The Runwell has been engineered with an obsessive focus to ensure the highest possible quality.
